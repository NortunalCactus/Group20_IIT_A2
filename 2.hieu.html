1 . What does it do?

Block Chain

First of all, in other to understand what Blockchain is, we need to understand what it is designed to solve in the first place. What I mean is that to tell a thing is fake or legit in our world, for example, our ID, license from car, bike to Certificate of educational attainment, or votes in the election we would keep a record of it. This leads to a potential problem that all centralized ledger have in common is that authorities could change the fact or history in the ledger for a not good purpose. As a result, we create and use Blockchain to solve these problems.

So what is Blockchain? Block-chain is a network of ledgers ( record book ) that replicated itself ( by hash the present and the previous block ) when we made a change and are designed to be decentralized and distributed across all computers that participate in the network. The benefits of using "decentralized ledger" rather than "centralized ledger" is that is removed the problems of power authorities in the system since everybody is equally privileged. Furthermore, All computers in the network must verify every change. Because of a secure system, there are no intermediaries such as the government or third parties.
Moreover, Blockchain, the first real implementation of this technology, is Bitcoin ( cryptocurrencies ), which was really successful in the recent period. Shortly, some of the application might apply Blockchain in the near future are:
- The "smart contract" when people have to make a new contract for different purpose likes real estate or economic contracts.
- "Medical record" since nowadays different hospitals have a different for each patient.
- "Election" since it could provide the transparency of the votes.

Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrency is a type of currency that uses digital files ( blockchain technology base ) as money; the most successful one worth mentioning is bitcoin.
To explain things, better we could see Bitcoin as digital gold:
- When gold is mining with pickaxes, bitcoin is mining with computer hardware ( CPU- Both of them are not controlled by the central bank and can be sent directly from user to user (decentralized and peer to peer cash system)
- Similar to gold each bitcoin cannot be replicated, and both of them have limited amounts on earth ( 21million bitcoin to be exact), and because of “blockchain”, all transition are verified and recorded by every computer that process bitcoin, so it is impossible to counterfeit a bitcoin.

However, different types of Cryptocurrency could have distinct features. For example, some would use different has functions like scrypt ( Litecoin ) rather than SHA-256 (Bitcoin), increased the size of each block for faster transitions ( Bitcoin Cash), some could make a transition without disclosing the sender, receiver or the amount transacted ( Zcash ).
